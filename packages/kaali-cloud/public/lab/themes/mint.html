FAQ
Common questions.
What is Starvox?▾
Starvox is a self-hosted Master AI Company OS — 16 specialist agents working in parallel to take your intent and ship production code, automatically.
How is this different from Copilot or Cursor?▾
Copilot assists you in your editor. Starvox replaces the full delivery loop — spec, code, test, deploy, monitor — no developer in the loop needed.
Which AI models does Starvox use?▾
25 models across 10 providers — Claude, GPT-4o, Gemini, DeepSeek, Mistral and more. The router picks the best model per sub-task by cost, speed, and capability.
Is my code safe?▾
Starvox is fully self-hosted. Code never leaves your machine. The sandbox is an isolated container on your own infrastructure. We see nothing.
Can I use it on existing products?▾
Yes. Point Starvox at any existing repo and describe a task. Agents read your project context before writing or deploying anything.
