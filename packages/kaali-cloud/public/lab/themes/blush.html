Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Starvox.

What is Starvox? +
Starvox is an AI operating system for companies. It deploys specialized agents that automate workflows, manage tasks, and help your team ship faster.
How do I get started? +
Sign up for a free Starter plan — no credit card required. You'll get three agents and a pipeline to experiment with immediately.
Can I customize agents? +
Yes. Pro and Enterprise plans allow you to train agents on your own data, define custom workflows, and integrate with any API.
Is my data secure? +
Absolutely. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Enterprise customers get a dedicated instance with SSO and audit logs.
